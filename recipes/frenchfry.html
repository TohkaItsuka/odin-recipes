French Fry Recipe

Description

This is a recipe on how to make homemade crispy seasoned french fries. With this recipe, you won't be able to get enough!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water, or as needed

Steps

  1. Slice potatoes into French Fries and place them into a large bowl of cold water to prevent them from turning brown.
  2. Heat oil in a heavy saucepan or large skillet to 350 degrees F.
  3. While the oil is heating, sift flour, garlic slt, paprika, and salt into a large bowl. Gradually stir in enough water to make a slightly thick batter that can be drizzled from a spoon.
  4. Drain fries and pat dry; dip into the batter one at a time and place in hot oil to prevent them from clumping together. Fry until golden brown and crispy on all sides, about 7-10 minutes; drain on a paper towal-lined plate.
